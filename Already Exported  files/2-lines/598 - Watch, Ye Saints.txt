598 - Watch, Ye Saints

Verse 1
Watch, ye saints, with eyelids waking;
Lo! The powers of heaven are shaking;

Verse 1
Keep your lamps all trimmed an burning, 
Ready for your Lord's returning.

Chorus
Lo! He comes, lo! Jesus comes;
Lo! He come, He comes all glorious!
