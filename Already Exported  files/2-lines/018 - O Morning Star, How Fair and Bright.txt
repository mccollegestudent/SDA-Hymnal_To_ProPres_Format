Verse 1
Our Lord and Master, You have won
Our hearts to serve You only!

Verse 1
Lowly, holy!
Great and Glorious, all victorious,

Verse 1

Verse 2

Your Word and Spirit, flesh and blood,

Alleluia! Christ the living,

Verse 4

Sing out! Ring out!
