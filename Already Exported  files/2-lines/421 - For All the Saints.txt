Verse 1
For all the saints
who from their labors rest,

Verse 1
Who thee by faith
before the world confessed.

Verse 1
Thy name, O Jesus,
be forever blest.

Verse 1

Alleluia!

Thou wast their Rock,
their Fortress and their Might;

Verse 2
Thou, Lord, their captain
in the well-fought fight;

Verse 2
Thou, in the darkness drear,
their one true light.

Verse 2
Alleluia!
Alleluia!

Verse 3
O may Thy soldiers,
faithful, true, and bold,

Verse 3
Fight as the saints
who nobly fought of old,

Verse 3
And win with them
the victor's crown of gold.

Verse 3
Alleluia!
Alleluia!

Verse 4
And when the strife is fierce,
the warfare long,

Verse 4
Steals on the ear
the distant triumph song,

Verse 4
And hearts are brave again,
and arms are strong.

Verse 4
Alleluia!
Alleluia!

Verse 5
From earth's wide bounds,
from ocean's farthest coast,

Verse 5
Thro' gates of pearl
streams in the countless host,

Verse 5
Singing to Father,
Son, and Holy Ghost,

Verse 5
Alleluia!
Alleluia!
