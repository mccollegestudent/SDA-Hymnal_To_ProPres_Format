071 - Come Thou Almighty King

Verse 1
Come, Thou almighty King,
Help us Thy name to sing,
Help us to praise!

Verse 1
Father all glorious,
O'er all victorious,
Come, and reign over us,

Verse 1
Ancient of Days!

Verse 2
Come, Thou incarnate Word,
Gird on Thy mighty sword,
Our prayer attend;

Verse 2
Come, and Thy people bless,
And give Thy Word success;
Spirit of holiness,

Verse 2
On us descend!

Verse 3
Come, holy Comforter,
Thy sacred witness bear,
In this glad hour:

Verse 3
Thou who almighty art,
Now rule in every heart,
And ne'er from us depart,

Verse 3
Spirit of power!

Verse 4
To Thee, great One in Three,
Eternal praises be,
Hence, evermore:

Verse 4
Thy sovereign majesty
May we in glory see,
And to eternity

Verse 4
Love and adore!
